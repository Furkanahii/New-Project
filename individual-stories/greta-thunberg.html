Greta Thunberg

A Young Voice Leading Global Climate Action

Updated 2024 By Emma Wilson 18 min read

From a solitary protest outside the Swedish parliament to inspiring millions worldwide, Greta Thunberg has become the face of youth climate activism. Her unwavering dedication to addressing the climate crisis has sparked a global movement and challenged world leaders to take immediate action.

"You are never too small to make a difference."
— Greta Thunberg

Early Life and Awakening

Born in Stockholm, Sweden, in 2003, Greta Thunberg learned about climate change at a young age. The reality of the climate crisis affected her deeply, leading to a period of depression. However, she transformed her concern into action, beginning with changes in her own lifestyle and family practices.
